Canada Visa

Documents Requirements (Tourist):

  • All Passports (required)
  • 2 passport sized photograph 35mm x 45mm white background or color
  • National ID card / smart card
  • Copy of marriage certificate
  • Bank Solvency Certificate and Bank Statement for the last six months company and personal both (required after appointment)
  • FDR papers (if any)
  • Assets papers based on Dhaka
  • If you are visiting a friend or family: A letter of invitation and supporting documents Canada -
  • a) stating the purpose and duration of visit
  • b) proof of relation to your inviter in Canada (birth certificate, marriage certificate, proof of correspondence etc)
  • c) Copy of inviter’s citizenship or immigration status document (for example: Permanent Resident Card – please photocopy both sides, Study Permit, Work Permit, etc.)
  • For tourism hotel booking
  • If you are employed, a signed original letter on company letterhead from the employer granting leave of absence and including:
  • a) the applicant’s name, position, current salary and date of hire
  • b) the employer’s name and address as well as the telephone and fax number
  • c) Income tax Certificate
  • d) Pay slips for the last six months
  • For Business person: Trade license (received), TIN Certificate , Visiting Card and other business related papers (business registration indicating your shares in the business, business license and financial documents, for example: profit and loss statement, balance sheet, etc.)
  • If you are a student, an original letter from your school confirming that the school has approved your absence and indicating when you are required to return and student ID, Birth Certificate
  • Visa fee : 11,480/-
  • Ser. Charge : 3,000/-
  • Time : Normally takes 22 working days
